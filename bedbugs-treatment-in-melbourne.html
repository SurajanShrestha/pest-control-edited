These pests are among the most feared mostly by homeowners and unfortunately, they are on the rise here in Melbourne and all over Australia.

Bed bugs are nocturnal insects capable of piercing skin and sucking up blood. These pests prefer human blood to other mammals but won’t stall to bite another mammal if human blood isn’t available. Although bed bug bites aren’t that harmful to humans, the effects of bed bug bites can cause serious health concerns like anaemia from excess loss of blood. Bed bug control is mandatory because some people have suffered from insomnia, fear, stress, anxiety and even paranoia from bed bug bites.
