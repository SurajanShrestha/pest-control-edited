Effects of Ants at Your Home/Office

Ants invade restaurants, hospitals, offices, warehouses, and other buildings where they can find food and water. Ant infestations are difficult to get rid of and require a trained professional. One ant finds out a good source of food and leaves a scent for other ants to follow and within a couple of minutes, an entire swarm of ants arrive. Ants like carpenter ants, can gradually damage your home whereas Fire Ants can cause immense amount of pain and leave the person on their knees. Things can become quite ugly if ant infestation is left untreated.

Ants will search for any source of food and water and march down on your toilet, trash can, and kitchen sink which may have hamful bacteria. They transmit these bacteria throughout the whole home which can cause people mostly Elders and Children to get in contact with it and suffer from serious illness. Ants can bring with them any number of other food-borne diseases like Shigella, clostridium, salmonella, staph, strep, E. coli, and various fungi. If ants gain access to your food they can transmit the bacteria they have been carrying almost instantaneously which can be severe.



Ant Bites can also cause infections and allergic reactions. Just a couple of bites can be enough to cause some serious itching, burning, and swelling. Bites from ants like Fire ants can cause the bite victim to be vulnerable to skin infections like cellulitis.
