Effects of Possums at Your Home/Office

Possums are wild animals that could nest inside your house/surroundings and produce a lot of offsprings which can be even a bigger hassle to deal with. They are known to excrete a lot of waste materials, mess with your house pets, eat your food and basically give birth to more possums which can be upto 10 times the trouble you expected.

Possums also spread harmful diseases especially from their feaces. Diseases like Tuberculosis is infamous from being spread from a Possum.
